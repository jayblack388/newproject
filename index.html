About Me

I grew up in Bozeman, Montana, so I have a deep appreciation for nature. I grew up hiking, fly fishing, horseback riding, skiing and snowboarding.

I moved to Oregon when I was 10, so when people ask me where I'm from, I still say Oregon. I have family members in MN, and moved here for college. Then I stayed for grad school. And I'm still here! In college I majored in Pre-med studies and Anthropology. My master's degree in is Medical Device Innovation. I am currently patenting my first medical device. I still plan to attend medical school, and hope to create medical devices as well.

I love traveling, learning languages, volunteering, and am passionate about racial justice issues, supporting women and minorities in business, being an ally to the LGBT community, and entrepreneurship overall.

I've been sky diving. I'm left handed and ambiextrous in sports. I make really good chocolate chip cookies. :)
